How to buy

1.

Summon Your Wallet: Start by creating a wallet with Phantom. Visit Phantom.app and set up a new account ​using the Phantom app or browser extension.

2.

Gather Your $SOL: Acquire some $SOL by tapping the BUY button within the app to purchase Solana, or ​deposit $SOL into your Phantom wallet from a crypto exchange of your choice.


3.

Swap for $WIZ: Transform your $SOL into $WIZ. Click on the SWAP icon in your Phantom wallet, enter ​the $WIZ token address, and trade your $SOL for Wizard Cat magic.

4.

Welcome to the Wizard Cat Coven!: Congratulations! You are now a proud member of the Wizard Cat Coven, ​ready to embark on an enchanted journey with $WIZ!
